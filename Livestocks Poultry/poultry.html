3 Keys to Poultry Success

Ideal Environment

  • Temperature:
    • 24–32°C (use shade nets, fans, or open-sided coops)
  • Ventilation:
    • Elevated coops + bamboo slats for airflow.
  • Rainy season:
    • Cover coops, avoid muddy floors to prevent worms.

Proper Enclosures

  • Native chickens:
    • Free-range (1 sqm/bird in coops at night).
  • Broilers:
    • Reduce density to 0.75 sq ft/bird (heat stress risk).
  • Shade:
    • Use coconut leaves/tarps to lower coop temperature.

Feed & Nutrition

  • Starter feed: 20% protein (add ipil-ipil leaves as supplement).
  • Layer feed: Crushed oyster shells or kuhol (snails) for calcium.
  • Water: Always cool + add electrolytes (e.g., Vitasol) in heatwaves.
