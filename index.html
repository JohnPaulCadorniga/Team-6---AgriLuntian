ABOUT US

AgriLuntian is an organization rooted in the fertile lands of Bulacan, combining “Agri” (agriculture) and “Luntian” (green) — a name that symbolizes growth, life, and sustainability. Inspired by the province’s rich farming heritage, AgriLuntian reflects the dedication of Bulakenyo farmers and their deep cultural connection to the land.

As both a digital platform, agricultural manufacturer, and distributor, AgriLuntian empowers Bulacan’s farming communities by providing easy access to real-time data, essential services, and localized agricultural knowledge. We bridge the gap between farmers and support institutions, while also processing, packaging, and distributing their crops and livestock — ensuring that high-quality agricultural products reach broader markets efficiently and sustainably.
