3 Keys to Piggery Success

Ideal Environment

  • Ventilation: Ensure cross-ventilation (1.5m exhaust fans per 20 pigs).
  • Temperature: Maintain 18–22°C (use misters in summer, straw bedding in winter).
  • Sanitation: Concrete floors with 3° slope for drainage + daily waste removal.

Proper Nutrition

  • Starter feeds: 20% protein for piglets (add probiotics for gut health).
  • Grower feeds: 16% protein + fiber (e.g., crushed corn, soybean meal).
  • Water: 2–3L per kg of feed; add electrolytes during heat stress.

Health Management

  • Day 7: Iron shot
  • Week 4: Deworming
  • Week 8: Hog cholera vaccine
